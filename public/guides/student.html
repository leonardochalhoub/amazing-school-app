Chapter 03 · Doing a lesson

Seven kinds of
exercise.

Listen & fill

Type the missing word

Plays a short clip. You write what you heard.

Translate line

English → Portuguese

Your teacher reviews. Save drafts freely.

Unscramble

Tap words in order

Build the sentence. Reset if you get stuck.

Cloze choice

Pick from options

Multi-choice with 4 tiles. Instant feedback.

Count word

How many times?

A quick number-entry exercise on a recording.

Line order

Number the lines

Re-sort a shuffled scene by time of appearance.

Each saved answer earns XP. Wrong answers still save — your teacher sees your attempts, not just your score.

Chapter 04 · Speaking lab

The robot that
hears you.

"Tap the mic. Say the sentence. The robot tells you exactly which word came out wrong."

How the score works

Fifteen parallel Groq Whisper models listen. They vote on what you said. Each word gets a clarity band: clean unclear suspicious.

How to practice

Press Play to hear the native line. Repeat it aloud. Press Stop. Compare. Press the ↻ button and try again. 30 attempts cost nothing — only your own time.

Chapter 05 · AI tutor

Ask anything.
Any hour.

Groq · llama-3.3-70b

Built for your level

It already knows you're a Brazilian learning US English. Corrects sentences, explains idioms, quizzes you when you ask. Answers in under a second.

Your teacher reads it

No secret channel

Every question you ask is logged in your teacher's view. This is a feature, not a bug. Come to class already sharper, not already corrected.

Good prompts: "Give me 3 synonyms for 'improve' with example sentences". "Quiz me on past simple irregular verbs, 5 questions, correct me after each one".

Chapter 06 · Music

Songs as
homework.

Every song in the catalog has a YouTube embed, a Letras.mus.br translation link, sing-along prompts, and 30 exercises built around the lyrics. Listen-fill, line-order, translate, discuss.

  • Click the CC button on the player for synced captions.
  • Click a sing-along prompt to jump to that exact timestamp in the video.
  • Click any vocab chip to open its Cambridge Portuguese entry.
  • Read the translation on Letras.mus.br when the lyrics get hard.

Chapter 08 · Privacy

Your data,
your rules.

What your teacher sees

Progress + activity, not transcripts

Your teacher sees XP, lesson completions, and how often you talk to the AI tutor — never the conversation itself. AI chats stay between you and the tutor. Your diary is teacher-authored notes about you, not your notes. We never sell your data or train AI on your conversations. Tap "How your data is handled" on your Profile for the full disclosure.

You control your photo

Upload, replace, or delete

Visible to your teacher and classmates only. If you prefer a cartoon, leave it blank — we pick one that matches your age and gender automatically.

Want everything deleted? Send one email. We wipe it within 72 hours.

Chapter 09 · Last piece of advice

Ten minutes a day
beats three hours on Sunday.

The streak counter isn't there to guilt you. It's there because a brain learns a language better in short, daily bursts. Do one drill before bed, one song before lunch. That's the whole trick.
